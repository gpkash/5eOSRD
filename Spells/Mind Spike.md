### Mind Spike
*2nd-level divination*

**Casting Time:** 1 Action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

You reach into the mind of one creature you can see within range. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw, taking 3d8 psychic damage on a failed save, or half as muc h damage on a suc c essful one. On a failed save, you also always know the target's location until the spell ends, but only while the two of you are on the same plane of existenc e. While you have this knowledge, the target can’t become hidden from you, and if it’s invisible, it gains no benefit from that condition against you.

***At higher level***: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.

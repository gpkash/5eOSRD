#### Armor of Resistance

Generic variant, rare (requires attunement), major tier

---

You have resistance to one type of damage while you wear this armor. The DM chooses the type or determines it randomly from the options below.

| d10 | Damage Type |
|:---:|-------------|
|  1  | *Acid*      |
|  2  | *Cold*      |
|  3  | *Fire*      |
|  4  | *Force*     |
|  5  | *Lightning* |
|  6  | *Necrotic*  |
|  7  | *Poison*    |
|  8  | *Psychic*   |
|  9  | *Radiant*   |
|  10 | *Thunder*   |

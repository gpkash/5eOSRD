## Long Rest in Armor

Attempting to sleep in armor risks exhaustion.

 If a player takes a long rest without doffing their armor, their night is restless and uncomfortable.

On a failed CON Saving Throw, add 1 level of Exhaustion.

| Armor  | DC  |
| ------ | --- |
| None   | 0   |
| Light  | 5   |
| Medium | 10  |
| Heavy  | 15  |

#### Fire Balm
Uncommon, Ointment

An orange, herbal paste used to aid people recovering from ailments. Applying a dose of this paste gives the user a +3 on a single [[Constitution]], [[Wisdom]] or [[Charisma]] saving throw for the next 14 hours.

### Slithering Tracker Stuffed Pastry

Recipe Price: 500 gp

This type of ooze can be injected into a pastry, but one has to be careful to not let the semi-conscious remains near one's mouth.

- ﻿﻿Special Ingredients: 1 [[Slithering Tracker]] ectoplasm (1 unit)
- ﻿﻿Required Spices: 1 gp
- ﻿﻿Portions Created: 3
- ﻿﻿Weight / Portion: 0.5 pound
- ﻿﻿Cooking DC: 14
- ﻿﻿Failure Effect: The cook begins to suffocate and choke.  
    The cook is able to make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns to end the effect
- ﻿﻿Effect: A creature who consumes a portion of this meal has advantage on Wisdom checks to track prey for 1 hour

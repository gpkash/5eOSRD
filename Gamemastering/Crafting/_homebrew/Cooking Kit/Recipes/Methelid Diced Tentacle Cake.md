### Methelid Diced Tentacle Cake

Recipe Price: 2,000 gp

A neothelid's tentacles can be diced and put into a cake alongside some apples. The apples reduce the acidity level, allowing a person to not die upon digesting this deliciously strange dessert.

- ﻿﻿Special Ingredients: 2 [[Neothelid]] Tentacles (2 units)
- ﻿﻿Required Spices: 8 gp
- ﻿﻿Portions Created: 4
- ﻿﻿Weight / Portion: 2 pounds
- ﻿﻿Cooking DC: 24
- ﻿﻿Failure Effect: The cook takes 35 (10d6) acid damage
- ﻿﻿Effect: A creature who consumes a portion of this meal gains the following ability for 10 minutes: it is aware of the presence of creatures within 500 feet of it that have an Intelligence score of 6 or higher. It knows the distance and direction to each creature, as well as the creature's Intelligence score, but can't sense anything else about it. A creature protected by a mind blank spell, a nondetection spell, or similar magic can't be perceived in this manner

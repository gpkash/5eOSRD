### Minced Mimic Pie

Recipe Price: 500 gp

While a mimic may seem like an inanimate object, it does in fact have flesh. A mimic can be made into a meat pie with some work and an oven.

- ﻿﻿Special Ingredients: 4 pounds of [[Mimic]] meat
- ﻿﻿Required Spices: 4 gp
- ﻿﻿Portions Created: 4
- ﻿﻿Weight / Portion: 2 pounds
- ﻿﻿Cooking DC: 13
- ﻿﻿Failure Effect: The cook polymorphs into an inanimate object of the DM's choice for 8 hours.
- ﻿﻿Effect: A creature who consumes a portion of this meal polymorphs into an inanimate object of their choice for 1 hour and can end this effect with an action.

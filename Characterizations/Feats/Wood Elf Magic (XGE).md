### Wood Elf Magic (XGE)

_Prerequisite: Elf (wood)_

---

You learn the magic of the primeval woods, which are revered and protected by your people. You learn one druid cantrip of your choice. You also learn the _longstrider_ and _pass without trace_ spells, each of which you can cast once without expending a spell slot. You regain the ability to cast these two spells in this way when you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for all three spells.

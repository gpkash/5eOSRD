### Critter Friend

_Prerequisite: Gnome (Forest)_

Your friendship with animals mystically deepens. You gain the following benefits:

- You gain proficiency in the Animal Handling skill. If you’re already proficient in it, your proficiency bonus is doubled for any check you make with it.
- You learn the _speak with animals_ spell and can cast it at will, without expending a spell slot. You also learn the _animal friendship_ spell, and you can cast it once with this feat, without expending a spell slot. You regain the ability to cast it in this way when you finish a long rest. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

Source: Unearthed Arcana
